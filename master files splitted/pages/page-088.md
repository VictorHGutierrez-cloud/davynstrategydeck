Security at Factorial
Your data is safe with us
At Factorial, safeguarding our clients' data is our top priority. We understand that 
trust is built on protecting information, so we implement rigorous measures to 
ensure its integrity, conﬁdentiality, and availability.
To achieve this, we have developed a robust Information Security Management 
System (ISMS), aligned with the international standard ISO/IEC 27001. This 
system enables us to identify, assess, and mitigate risks, ensuring operational 
continuity and meeting our security objectives.
In this section, we will explore in detail the practices and controls we apply to 
protect data, minimize threats, and provide a secure environment for all our 
clients.
